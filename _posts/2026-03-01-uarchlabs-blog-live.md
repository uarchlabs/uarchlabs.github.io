The uarchlabs blog is live. Posts covering the design and implementation of Pacino — an 8-issue out-of-order RVA23S64 RISC-V processor — will appear here.

The first series covers the branch prediction unit: cluster architecture, history and uBTB, the loop predictor, and lessons from the co-design process.
